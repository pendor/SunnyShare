About Sunny+Share

Like a cache of treasures left behind in the forrest, this is a place of digital artifacts of others who have visited this place before you.

Sunny+Share is a (usually) solar powered anonymous sharing device. You're invited to place photos, media, and other digital content that others in this area may find interesting. Uploads are visible to anyone within WiFi range, but are NOT accessible on the public internet. When you leave this place, like footprints in the forrest, what you share stays behind.

Access to this system is anonymous. No logs are kept. No personal information is recorded. Once uploaded, files can only be removed by this device's owner. Please only share content that is appropriate for everyone in this area to view and be mindful of copyright & other applicable laws. Inappropriate or unlawful content will be removed. This service may disappear at any time. Use it at your own risk. No warranty of any kind is expressed or implied. Uploads may be deleted at any time.

Sunny+Share is built on Free Software, including Linux, PHP, and miniDLNA. These programs are included under a variety of Open licenses including GPLv2, MIT, BSD, etc. Sunny+Share itself is released under GPL version 3 only.
