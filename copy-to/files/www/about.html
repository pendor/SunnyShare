About Sunny+Share

Sunny+Share is a (frequently) solar powered anonymous information sharing device. You are invited to share photos, media, and other digital content that others in this area may find interesting. Uploads are visible to anyone within WiFi range, but are NOT accessible on the public Internet. All access to this system is completely anonymous. No logs are kept nor is any personal information recorded. Please be mindful of copyright and other applicable laws. Inappropriate or unlawful content will be removed.

Built on Free Software, Suny+Share facilitates local, anonymous sharing digital content. No logins are required and no user data is logged. The system is not connected to the Internet in order to prevent tracking and preserve privacy.

Once uploaded, files can only be removed by this device's owner. Please only share content that is appropriate for everyone in this area to view. This service may disappear at any time. Use it at your own risk. No warranty of any kind is expressed or implied. Uploaded content may be deleted at any time.

Sunny+Share is based on PirateBox and OpenWRT. Sunny+Share as a whole is licensed under GPLv3.
